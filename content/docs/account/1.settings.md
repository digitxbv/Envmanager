# Account Settings

Your account settings let you view your profile information and manage your security settings like changing your password.

## Accessing Account Settings

::steps
### Open Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar navigation from any dashboard page.

### Select Profile Tab

The Profile tab is selected by default, showing your personal account settings.
::

![Account Settings - Profile tab showing account information and change password form](/images/docs/account/account-settings-profile.png)

*The Settings page with the Profile tab selected, showing your account information and password change form.*

## Viewing Account Information

The Profile tab displays your basic account information:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Email Address** | The email you use to sign in |
| **Member Since** | The date you created your account |

::callout{icon="i-lucide-info" color="info"}
Your email address is used for signing in and receiving notifications. Contact support if you need to change your email address.
::

## Changing Your Password

You can update your password at any time from the Settings page.

::steps
### Navigate to Profile Settings

Go to **Settings** and ensure the **Profile** tab is selected.

### Find Change Password Section

Scroll down to the "Change Password" section.

### Enter New Password

Type your new password in the "New Password" field.

### Confirm Password

Re-enter the same password in the "Confirm New Password" field.

### Update

Click **Update Password** to save your new password.
::

::callout{icon="i-lucide-check-circle" color="success"}
Your password is updated immediately. Use your new password the next time you sign in.
::

### Password Requirements

Your password must:
- Be at least 6 characters long
- Match in both password fields

::callout{icon="i-lucide-lightbulb"}
Use a password manager to generate and store a strong, unique password for your EnvManager account.
::

## Security Best Practices

### Protect Your Account

- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your login credentials
- Sign out when using shared computers
- Enable 2FA when available

### Monitor Access

- Review your organization memberships periodically
- Check which environments you have access to
- Report suspicious activity to your organization admin

### Handle Credentials Carefully

- Never share secret values through insecure channels
- Use EnvManager's sharing features instead of copying secrets
- Report if you suspect credentials have been compromised

## Getting Help

- **Forgot password?** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the sign-in page
- **Can't access your account?** Contact your organization admin or support
- **Need to change email?** Contact support
